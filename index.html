Why PorchDrop?

Stop finding dinner on the ground. PorchDrop is a compact, weather-resistant platform that tells drivers exactly where to set your order — with “PLACE FOOD HERE” embossed on top.

  • Snap-lock folding legs for fast setup
  • Stable on typical porch surfaces
  • Low-profile storage when folded

At a Glance

Top: 16" × 16" (prototype) • Height: ~18" (min) • Weather-resistant materials
Models: Classic (fixed legs) • Fold (U-frame / X-frame concept)
